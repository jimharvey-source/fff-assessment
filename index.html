About this assessment. The FFF Presentation Skills Assessment is a self-report development tool designed to support reflection and professional growth. Your results reflect your own perceptions at the time of taking. They are not a fixed or clinical verdict.

The tool is in active development. It draws on more than 30 years of presentation coaching practice and the Fit, Focus and Flair competency framework. It has not yet been through formal psychometric validation. Results should be used as a starting point for development, not as a definitive measure of ability.

The tool is not intended for use in recruitment, selection, or performance management. If you are a researcher or practitioner interested in collaborating on validation work, contact jim.harvey@themessagebusiness.com
